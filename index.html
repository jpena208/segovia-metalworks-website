About Us

Segovia Metal Works LLC is a family owned and operated company that designs and sells customized metal products (barbecue pits, firepits, trailers, log racks, benches, tables, trailer pits, custom logos/names and many other barbecuing accessories).

Browse Our Shop

Experienced and Reliable

We have over 20 years experience in oilfield, construction, sandblasting and painting and over 10 years of welding experience. Every one of our products are individually inspected making sure you receive quality work all done at an affordable price!!

Customer Satisfaction

Located in Weslaco, TX, all of our products are welded, sandblasted and painted right here in the RGV by a team you can trust with quality work and products.

Payment can be made with credit card or Paypal

Some orders are available for delivery, but other orders need to be picked up from the shop.

  • Name on pit is extra $15 (4x12)
  • Logo on pit is extra $25 (10x16) max
  • Add color to logo or name is $5 extra
  • Custom grills or pits:
    16” pit is $100,
    20” pit is 150 ,
    24” pit is $200

    (Custom grills are 3/16 thick and can be done with any name and logo)

Mobile welding available at $70 per working hour with a minimum of two hours. Additionally, travel hours will be charged as working hours.

Example:
A two hour trip is two hours of travel pay at $70 per hour plus time spent working at location.

In shop jobs are $55 an hour (not including material needed for the job).
