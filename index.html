About Us

Segovia Metal Works llc is a family owned and operated company that designs and sells customized metal products ( barbecue pits, firepits, trailers, log racks, benches, tables, trailer pits, custom logos/names and many other barbecuing accessories).

Experienced and Reliable

We have over 20 years experience in oilfield/construction, over 20 years in sandblasting and painting, over 10 years of welding experience. Every one of our products are inspected individually making sure the work you receive is quality work all done at an affordable price!!

Satisfaction Guranteed

Located in the RGV working out of Weslaco tx all of our products are welded, sandblasted and painted right here in the RGV by a team you can trust with quality work and products.

How Do I Purchase And Customize?

Here's how.

Call or text us at (956) 647-7174
Message us on facebook @segovia metal works
or email us at josedsegovia99@gmail.com

Payment is available through credit card and paypal
As well as cash or check in person at shop location

Some orders are available for delivery, other orders need to be picked up from the shop.

Send us this information with your order:

  • Name
  • Email/Phone Number
  • Address (If ordering items available for delivery)
  • Product Name
  • Customization Options (If applicable)

Extra Services

Mobile welding available at $70 per working hour with a minimum of two hours. Additionally, travel hours will be charged as working hours.
Example:

A two hour trip is two hours of travel pay at $70 per hour plus time spent working at location.

In shop jobs are $55 an hour (not including material needed for the job).

Custom Options

  • Name on pit is extra $15 (4x12)
  • Logo on pit is extra $25 (10x16) max
  • Add color to logo or name is $5 extra
  • Custom grills or pits:
    16” pit is $100,
    20” pit is 150 ,
    24” pit is $200

    (Custom grills are 3/16 thick and can be done with any name and logo)
